Graphic Design



Ever since my junior year, I have been a graphic designer for the MoCo Student, a newsletter informing the Montgomery County community. I am responsible for drawing pictures for articles to better visualize a topic. I enjoy being able to use Illustrator and Photoshop to create these images and attract more readers. I create graphic designs for online articles as well as cartoons that focus on global, regional, and school issues. In addition, I collaborate with multiple people in order to create successful newspapers and attract many readers. I inform students about different issues that are occuring in the community around us.
